Why Serverless?

At HackerPile, we're convinced that serverless architecture is the future of all web apps. Maybe that's a stretch, but a serverless approach definitely fit our business plan. Building HackerPile on a serverless backend eliminated barrier to entry of high-speed scalability. Using AWS Lambda with the API gateway allows for fast, seemless, and reliable data flow.
